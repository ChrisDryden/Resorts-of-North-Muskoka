Blue Spruce Resort    www.bluespruce.ca

Blue Spruce Resort is a spectacular fully equipped Cottage Resort and Motel, perfectly at home in nature's domain located in a world, simply, unto itself.

The largest fully equipped housekeeping resort on Oxtongue Lake located near the West Gate of Algonquin Park Ontario Canada.

Blue Spruce Resort is balanced on the edge of village and forest. It is a realm of infinite welcome and great beauty, where lakeview cottages with wood burning fireplaces embrace the magnificence of nature, and our own cross country skiing and hiking trails wander off into the quiet peaceful woods.

This comfortable vacation property borders Muskoka, Haliburton, and Algonquin Park, three of the most sought after vacation areas in Ontario's cottage country.

We are an all season, year round retreat that is perfect for a Family Vacation, a Romantic Getaway, or an Adventure Holiday. You will find an abundance of activities to enjoy in and around the lodge, although it's perfectly all right to do nothing at all. Whether you prefer to curl up in front of your wood burning fireplace or take to the lake and paddle a canoe or kayak to Ragged Falls - a wonderful half day adventure- Blue Spruce Resort is sure to please.

Tally Ho Inn    www.tallyhoinn.com

Tally Ho Inn is a welcoming Muskoka cottage resort that is family and pet friendly. Located on the shores of Peninsula lake just outside of Huntsville and minutes from Algonquin Park, Tally Ho Inn offers you the perfect Muskoka getaway. With 1300 feet of shoreline and cottages ranging from one room suites, to 3 bedroom cottages with or without full kitchens, we have a little something for everyone. Have a pet? Don't worry, we are a pet friendly resort and welcome your furry family members. All our units have a microwave, coffee maker, fridge, and satellite TV. Whether you're looking to sit back, relax and reconnect with your family or looking for some adventure, there is something for everyone. The Pines Cottage Resort    www.algonquinparkaccommodations.com

The Pines Cottage Resort is a small enclave of fully equipped housekeeping cottages on a plateau overlooking a large, safe, sandy beach on Oxtongue Lake complete with anchored inflatable water toys. All cottages at the Pines will have been outfitted with updated beds, furniture, wood or laminate floors, linens, towels and kitchen equipment. Also provided for your convenience are picnic tables, patio furniture, BBQ, cleaning brush, ash tray, canoes, kayaks, paddleboats, lifejackets, bail buckets and paddles. Enjoy the social interaction of the community fire each evening.

Our proximity to Algonquin Park means your stay at The Pines can be as active or as relaxing as you would like it. Take in all that Algonquin Park has to offer. Nature trails in this area and in the Park, Algonquin's world class Visitor's Centre, the Algonquin Park Logging Museum, the Algonquin Art Gallery combine to allow you to make your stay at The Pines truly memorable.

The Pines Cottage Resort has a long standing reputation for its friendly atmosphere. Over the years guests have made friendships with other guests which extend to socializing with each other throughout the entire year. The Pines Cottage Resort is associated with Camp Tanamakoon for girls and Bartlett Lodge, a Modified American Plan Resort. Spend an evening of fine dining at the Pines sister resort Bartlett Lodge. Both of these businesses are located in the heart of Algonquin Park and have been operated by the Kim and Marilyn Smith for over 12 years. The Smiths' experience in the resort industry and their knowledge of the local area means The Pines Cottage Resort was build on its already strong reputation as a quiet friendly retreat on the doorstep of Algonquin Park.
