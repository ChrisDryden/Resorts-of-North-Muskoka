General Driving Directions

From Toronto
Traveling from the Toronto area follow highway 400 north to highway # 11. Continue north on highway 11 to highway # 60. Highway #60 is the 3rd exit for Huntsville and travels eastward to Algonquin Park and Ottawa. Travel along highway #60 to Dwight.

From Ottawa
Take Hwy 60 through Dwight.

From North Bay, Sudbury
Travel to intersection Hwy 11 and Hwy 60. Go east on Hwy 60 to Dwight.
