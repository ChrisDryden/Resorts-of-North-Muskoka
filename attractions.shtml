No matter what time of year you visit, there is so much to do in North Muskoka and many exciting events that take place throughout the year. Rich in history and culture, North Muskoka is a popular year-round outdoor playground for young and old alike. A very special Ontario getaway destination that offers something for everyone, a North Muskoka vacation is sure to provide lasting memories of fun times, interesting sites and unique experiences.


North Muskoka Attractions and Entertainment
It can never be said that there is nothing to do in North Muskoka. With such a wide variety of interesting local attractions and entertainment options, your biggest problem when vacationing here will be deciding what to do next. For families with children, a trip to Santa's Village in Bracebridge is sure to please. For the history buff, a visit to the Bethune Memorial House, Muskoka Heritage Place and Pioneer Museum, the Muskoka Rails Museum or the Lake of Bays Marine Museum and SS Bigwin would be entertaining and educational. As well, there are a number of wonderful area boat tours, nature trails and scenic lookouts, shops, and so much more.

The Great Outdoors
North Muskoka is all about enjoying the great outdoors. A respite from the crowds and noise of the city, time spent in North Muskoka, where the air is fresh and the water is clean, can be rejuvenating for both body and soul. North Muskoka is indeed a special place, full of natural beauty that can be enjoyed in many ways; by sitting quietly by the lake or by hiking through the forest. Our many lakes and vast wilderness are ideal for sportsmen and nature lovers alike. Our many trails, including those in Arrowhead and Algonquin Parks, allow visitors to explore the magnificent landscape and the wild life that lives here. Here in North Muskoka, you can enjoy all manner of water sports; canoeing, kayaking, waterskiing, swimming. We have excellent fishing and hunting and we have a variety of challenging golf courses. Depending on the season, you can mountain bike, ski, hike, snowmobile, atv, horseback ride, snowshoe, or simply relax and enjoy the beautiful surroundings.

Golf
A very popular Ontario golf vacation destination, North Muskoka has over a dozen golf courses, including economical courses for families and beginners, challenging championship courses that test the skills of even the most experienced golfer, and golf courses for every skill level in between. North Muskoka's rugged landscape, carved out of the Canadian Shield creates a spectacular backdrop for a golf experience like no other. Whether you choose to golf at one of our world-class, award-winning golf courses, like Taboo, The Rock, and Bigwin Island, or enjoy a quick and casual nine holes at a nearby par 3, you will find golfing in North Muskoka to be a unique and pleasurable experience.

Water Activities
Muskoka is synonymous with Ontario lake country. Here, we are blessed with many beautiful lakes, rivers and thousands of kilometres of shoreline. In large part, our magnificent waterways and the enjoyment they provide are what make North Muskoka the popular vacation destination that it is. On the Lake of Bays, Peninsula Lake, and on our many smaller lakes, visitors indulge in all types of water-based activities, such as swimming, canoeing, kayaking, wakeboarding, waterskiing, fishing, power boating and more. A variety of marine services, including fishing charters, power boat rentals and guided boat tours are also available.

Shopping
While vacationing in Muskoka, spend a little time to explore some of the many wonderful shops in our area. The main street in historic downtown Huntsville, has all the retail stores, shops and services you may require while on holiday in Muskoka. Here you will also find gift shops to find the perfect souvenir, antique stores, eclectic boutiques, and arts and craft shops featuring the work of local artisans. Even the small villages of Dorset, Dwight and Baysville offer a fun shopping experience with a few very unique specialty stores and gift shops that warrant a quick look see, as well as Canada's number #1 country general store.
