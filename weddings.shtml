Muskoka Wedding Venues & Group Event Facilities

Located just 2 ½ hours north of Toronto in the heart of cottage country, North Muskoka is the perfect location to hold a memorable group event or celebrate a special occasion with family and friends. The ideal venue for a destination wedding, family reunion, golf tournament, or girl's weekend away, the resorts of North Muskoka have so much to offer. When you hold your special group function at a North Muskoka resort, inn or lodge, you can be certain that you and your party will enjoy the very best in hospitality and exceptional service, whether you choose to hold your group event at one of our family-friendly housekeeping cottage resorts or at one of our fine lakeside inns or lodges. North Muskoka offers beautiful scenery, a variety of accommodation styles, good food, wide variety of activities and many fabulous attractions. If you are looking for a unique, yet romantic location to hold your wedding, look no further than North Muskoka. Our spectacular lakes and beautiful wilderness vistas will create an unforgettable backdrop for the Ontario resort wedding of your dreams.

For brides who want to turn their special day into a weekend or week-long celebration with friends and family, a destination wedding at a North Muskoka resort is ideal. Many of North Muskoka's lodges, inns and resorts can be reserved exclusively for the wedding party, family and guests, for a weekend or longer, giving the bridal couple more time to spend with family and friends and wedding guests the opportunity to enjoy a vacation in cottage country. A North Muskoka resort wedding is an affordable alternative to an expensive destination wedding in far-flung locales like the Caribbean.

For your next company function, why not combine business with pleasure at a North Muskoka resort, lakeside inn or lodge? Your company retreat will be a total success when you include recreation and fun. North Muskoka has a number of fine lodges inns and resorts that have small conference and meeting facilities, as well as superior accommodations and excellent dining to satisfy everyone in your group. There is a North Muskoka accommodation property with the facilities, guest rooms and amenities to host any type of corporate function or group event that you may wish to hold in beautiful Muskoka. Our outstanding resorts with banquet, meeting and conference facilities will work with you to ensure that your company event, meeting, conference, seminar, retreat or team-building session is a great success. The resorts of North Muskoka offer a relaxed and peaceful environment to conduct a successful and very productive business meeting, where the setting will be inspirational and the service warm and accommodating. And, after your work is done, your team can enjoy an extensive array of on-site recreational activities, as well as many nearby attractions including championship golf courses, downhill skiing, boat cruises, extensive trails and Algonquin Park. Ideal for team building and boosting company moral, a group event or small business function held at a North Muskoka resort allows you take care of business while rewarding your employees.
